Group Project

Project Information

  • Name: Healthcare Information System
  • Category: Web Application
  • Project date: 21 June, 2023
  • Language/framework used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Php, MySQL

The “Healthcare Information System” is a comprehensive software system designed to manage and store healthcare-related information, facilitating efficient data flow within a healthcare organization. It aims to improve patient care, enhance operational efficiency, and ensure secure and accurate medical data storage. It also provides a centralized repository for electronic health records, enabling real-time access and updates by healthcare providers. The Healthcare Information System helps healthcare organizations manage patients more efficiently by simplifying tasks like registering patients and keeping track of their medical history, medications, and laboratory history. It also automates billing and payment processes, making financial management more effortless. The system allows restriction of the data by only showing or accessing the needed information by the staff based on its position, making the patient's data secure. It also offers reports and analytics to gain insights and improve the quality of care. Implementing this system will improve workflows and efficiently manage the health history of the patient, which will enhance the quality of care provided.
